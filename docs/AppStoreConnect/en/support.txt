Circadia — Support

GETTING STARTED
1. On first launch, allow notifications and (optionally) Health access.
2. Tap Start in the evening, or use the Apple Watch app.
3. In the morning, fill in the short two-tap survey to personalize Circadia to you.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Why don't I see my sleep stages?
Circadia needs at least a few minutes of motion and (ideally) heart rate data to begin classifying. If you wear an Apple Watch, make sure the Circadia companion is running on the Watch.

How accurate is the smart alarm?
The smart alarm fires inside the window you choose, when Circadia estimates you are in light sleep. If you don't want to risk missing the alarm, set a separate iOS alarm as a backstop.

Where is my data?
On this device. Circadia does not have a server. You can delete everything from Settings → Local Data.

Will snore detection record me?
No. When you enable it, audio is processed into short numerical features in real time and discarded. Nothing is saved or transmitted.
