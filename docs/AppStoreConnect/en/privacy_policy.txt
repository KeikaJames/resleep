Circadia — Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026

Circadia is offline-first. Everything you record stays on your device by default.

WHAT STAYS LOCAL
- Heart rate, HRV, motion, and derived sleep stages.
- Session history, summaries, trends, and diagnostic logs.
- Any audio captured for snore detection (features only — see below).

WHAT WE NEVER DO
- We never record or upload raw audio. The microphone, when enabled, processes audio on-device into short numerical features and discards the audio immediately.
- We do not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- We do not run a backend for your data. There are no accounts.

HEALTHKIT
If you allow it, Circadia can read heart rate and HRV from HealthKit and write a sleep analysis sample back. You can revoke either at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

ON-DEVICE PERSONALIZATION
Circadia can adapt its stage detection to your wake-up surveys. The personalization model and the labels derived from your survey responses are stored only on this device, in the app's container.

DIAGNOSTICS
A local diagnostic log records events such as session start/stop and Watch reachability so you can review what happened overnight. The log lives in this app's container and can be cleared from Settings → Diagnostics.

CHILDREN
Circadia is not directed at children under 13.

DELETION
You can delete every byte stored by Circadia from Settings → Local Data → Delete Local Sleep Data, or by deleting the app.

CONTACT
Questions: contact the developer through the App Store listing.
