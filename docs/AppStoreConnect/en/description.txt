Sleep is a quiet sleep tracker that runs entirely on your iPhone and Apple Watch.

WHY SLEEP

· Offline first. Heart rate, motion, and breathing analysis all happen on this device. There is no server, no account, and no tracking.
· Privacy by default. Audio is never recorded and never uploaded. Snore detection works on transient acoustic features, then forgets them.
· Calm by design. One screen for tonight. One screen for trends. No streaks, no gamification, no ads.

WHAT YOU GET

· Automatic sleep stages. Light, Deep, REM, and Awake — estimated from heart rate, HRV, and motion using a small on-device model.
· Smart wake. The Apple Watch buzzes gently inside a window you choose, when you are in light sleep.
· Trends. A simple weekly score line and stage composition for the last 7 to 30 nights.
· Wake-up survey. A two-tap morning check-in that quietly personalizes the model to you over time.
· Sleep notes. Tag caffeine, alcohol, exercise, stress, travel, and more — see what affects your nights.

PRIVACY PROMISES

· No raw audio is saved or sent. Ever.
· No third-party analytics. No advertising SDKs.
· You can delete every byte from Settings at any time.

NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE

Sleep is for personal wellness. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Do not rely on the smart alarm if missing it would cause harm.

REQUIREMENTS

· iPhone with iOS 17 or later
· Apple Watch with watchOS 10 or later (optional but recommended)
