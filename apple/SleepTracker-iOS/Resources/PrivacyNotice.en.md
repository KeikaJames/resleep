# Circadia Privacy Notice

**Effective from the date this document is published or updated in the App.**
The latest version is the one displayed in-App.
**Provider: BIRI GA**
**Applies to: the Circadia mobile app and related features**

This *Circadia Privacy Notice* ("Notice") explains how BIRI GA handles information related to you when you use Circadia.

Please read this Notice carefully before using Circadia. By using Circadia you confirm you understand the information-handling practices described here.

## 1. Information we process

To provide, maintain and improve Circadia, BIRI GA may process:

1. information you input or generate in Circadia;
2. sleep records, sleep trends, sleep duration and related health-tracking data;
3. data you authorise Circadia to read from or write to Apple Health;
4. on-device-processed Q&A and contextual information when you use Sleep AI or other AI features;
5. feedback, support queries, error reports or other communications you actively submit;
6. technical, diagnostic, crash and performance information related to App run-time;
7. subscription state, purchase records and App Store receipt-validation information;
8. device model, system version, App version, language, region, timestamps and similar technical information;
9. other information processed under applicable law or with your separate authorisation.

## 2. Apple Health data

Circadia may request access to Apple Health data types relevant to your use of Circadia, including sleep and other health-related data.

Apple Health permissions are managed by iOS. You may grant, deny or revoke each data type independently.

Circadia only reads or writes Apple Health data within the scope you have granted. You may review, adjust or revoke these authorisations in iOS Settings at any time.

BIRI GA does not use Apple Health data for advertising, targeted marketing, sale to data brokers, or any purpose unrelated to Circadia features.

If you revoke Apple Health authorisations, some Circadia features may not work as expected, and the completeness and accuracy of data displays, statistics or AI responses may be affected.

## 3. Sleep AI information

The core Sleep AI Q&A functionality runs locally on your iPhone by default.

For the core Q&A functionality, the following is generally processed on-device:

1. your prompt or instruction;
2. the sleep data Sleep AI references;
3. the contextual data relevant to that exchange;
4. the response generated by the model.

Unless you actively share, export, submit feedback, contact support, or otherwise transmit relevant content, BIRI GA does not proactively upload Sleep AI core Q&A content to a developer server.

If a future version of Sleep AI introduces cloud models, remote inference, cross-device sync, server-side log analytics or any other capability that may cause core Q&A content to leave your device, BIRI GA will explain this prior to enabling and obtain your consent where applicable.

## 4. Crash reports, diagnostics and Apple analytics

When Circadia runs as an iOS App, Apple, iOS, or App Store Connect may, depending on your system settings, Apple policy and applicable service mechanisms, process crash reports, diagnostics, performance data, usage analytics or similar technical data related to Circadia.

Such data may include:

1. device model;
2. iOS version;
3. App version;
4. crash time;
5. crash thread and error logs;
6. performance metrics;
7. technical information related to App run-time state.

If you have opted in to share analytics with Apple or developers in iOS Settings, Apple may provide developers with analytics or diagnostics related to App crashes and run-time behaviour.

BIRI GA may use such information to investigate issues, fix bugs, improve App stability, ensure security and improve product experience.

BIRI GA does not ask you to actively submit full Sleep AI Q&A content in crash reports or diagnostics. However, because diagnostics and crash reports are processed by Apple and iOS system mechanisms, BIRI GA cannot guarantee that all technical logs, in every situation, are entirely free of fragmentary technical information related to your use of the App.

## 5. How we use information

BIRI GA may use information for the following purposes:

1. providing Circadia's basic features;
2. displaying sleep records, trends and related statistics;
3. reading or writing Apple Health within the scope you authorise;
4. providing Sleep AI or other AI-assisted features;
5. handling subscriptions, purchases, restore-purchase and related App Store flows;
6. investigating bugs, analysing crashes, improving stability and security;
7. responding to your feedback, queries or support requests;
8. preventing fraud, abuse, unlawful use or security risks;
9. complying with legal, regulatory, platform-policy or dispute-resolution requirements;
10. improving product experience where consent or another lawful basis applies.

## 6. When information may leave your device

Information may leave your device when:

1. you actively share, export, screenshot, copy or forward Circadia or Sleep AI content;
2. you submit feedback, support queries or error reports to BIRI GA;
3. you authorise Apple Health, iCloud, iOS, Apple, third-party Apps or third-party services to handle relevant content;
4. Apple, iOS or App Store Connect processes crash reports, diagnostics, performance data or similar technical data per your settings and Apple policy;
5. subscription, purchase, refund or App Store flows require it;
6. legal, regulatory, court order or other lawful processes require disclosure;
7. there is a reasonable need to protect the legitimate rights, safety or property of BIRI GA, users, Apple or third parties;
8. as listed in this Notice or otherwise authorised by you.

## 7. Sharing

BIRI GA does not sell your personal information or Apple Health data.

BIRI GA may share or receive relevant information in the following cases:

1. with Apple — for App distribution, subscriptions, purchases, crash reports, diagnostics, Apple Health permissions and iOS system features;
2. with service providers — if Circadia in the future uses cloud storage, customer support, analytics, crash diagnostics or other technical services, the necessary information will be processed within the applicable scope;
3. legal and compliance — to comply with applicable law, court orders, regulatory requirements or lawful process;
4. security and rights protection — to prevent fraud, abuse, security risks or to protect legitimate rights;
5. with your consent — when you have explicitly authorised or actively chosen to share.

If Circadia integrates with new third-party services in the future, BIRI GA will explain this in this Notice, App settings, the App Store privacy notice or another appropriate place.

## 8. Retention

The core Sleep AI Q&A content is processed and stored mainly on your device.

You may delete, manage or reset relevant content via the in-App settings or device settings, depending on the App version.

If you actively submit feedback, support queries or other content to BIRI GA, BIRI GA will retain such information for as long as needed to fulfil the relevant purpose, or as required by applicable law.

The retention and handling of crash reports, diagnostics, analytics, subscription records and similar technical data processed by Apple, iOS or App Store Connect may be determined by Apple's relevant policies and your system settings.

## 9. Security

BIRI GA takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect Circadia-related information.

You should also take reasonable measures to protect your device and information, including:

1. enabling device lock;
2. safeguarding your Apple ID and device password;
3. carefully managing Apple Health and other system permissions;
4. avoiding sharing Circadia or Sleep AI content in unsafe environments;
5. keeping the App and operating system updated;
6. avoiding inputting unnecessary sensitive personal information into Circadia or Sleep AI.

## 10. Sensitive information

Circadia may involve health and sleep-related information. You should avoid inputting sensitive information unrelated to Circadia features, including:

1. medical or health information of others;
2. ID numbers;
3. financial information;
4. precise addresses;
5. passwords or login credentials;
6. other unnecessary sensitive personal information.

If you input personal information of others, you must ensure you have obtained all necessary authorisations and consents.

## 11. Minors

Circadia is not intended for children under 13. If you are located in mainland China, Circadia is not intended for children under 14.

Minors using Circadia should obtain the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian.

Parents or guardians should remind minors to avoid inputting sensitive personal information, others' information or unnecessary health information.

## 12. Your rights

Under applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, or withdraw consent regarding your information.

You can manage some information and permissions via:

1. deleting, managing or resetting content in Circadia (depending on the App version);
2. managing Apple Health permissions in iOS Settings;
3. managing analytics-sharing, notifications, background refresh and other system permissions in iOS Settings;
4. managing subscriptions on the Apple ID subscriptions page;
5. contacting BIRI GA via the contact methods listed in this Notice.

To exercise privacy rights under applicable law, contact BIRI GA at:

## 13. Cross-border processing

Circadia mainly runs locally on your device.

When you actively submit feedback, contact support, use Apple services, App Store subscriptions, Apple Health, iCloud, crash reports or other third-party services, relevant information may be processed by Apple, BIRI GA or relevant service providers outside your region.

Cross-border processing will be carried out in accordance with applicable law, Apple policy and relevant service terms.

## 14. Updates

BIRI GA may update this Notice from time to time.

If a material change occurs, BIRI GA will notify you via in-app prompts, update notices or other appropriate means, and may require you to re-consent where applicable.

Continued use of Circadia after the updated Notice takes effect indicates that you understand the updated information-handling practices.
