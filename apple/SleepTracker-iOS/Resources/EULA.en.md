## 1. Nature of Sleep AI

Sleep AI is an AI-assisted feature inside Circadia that helps you make sense of general sleep trends, sleep habits and health-tracking data.

Sleep AI uses generative AI models to produce general responses based on your prompt, relevant Circadia data, and Apple Health data you have authorised Circadia to access.

Sleep AI output is for general information and health-tracking reference only.

## 2. What Sleep AI is for

Sleep AI is intended for general purposes including:

1. helping you understand sleep duration, patterns and trends;
2. providing non-clinical information about general sleep habits;
3. answering natural-language questions about your Circadia data;
4. generating general wellness tips and sleep-habit suggestions;
5. helping you review non-medical information about sleep, schedule and health tracking.

Sleep AI is not a medical service and must not be used in any medical, clinical, diagnostic, therapeutic or emergency situation.

## 3. Not a medical device, not medical advice

Sleep AI is not a medical device.

1. diagnose any disease, disorder, sleep condition or health state;

4. determine whether you need medical attention;
5. replace the opinion of a doctor, clinician, mental-health professional or other qualified professional.

## 4. On-device processing

The core question-and-answer functionality of Sleep AI runs locally on your iPhone by default.

For the core Q&A functionality, the following is generally processed on-device:

1. your prompt or instruction;
2. the sleep data Sleep AI references;
3. the contextual data relevant to that exchange;
4. the response generated by the model.

Unless you actively choose to share, export, submit feedback, contact support, or otherwise transmit relevant content, BIRI GA does not proactively upload Sleep AI core Q&A content to a developer server.

Apple Health permissions are managed by iOS. You may grant, deny or revoke each data type independently.

Sleep AI will only reference Apple Health data within the scope you have granted to Circadia.

If you revoke Apple Health authorisations, Sleep AI may have less data available, and the completeness and accuracy of generated responses may be affected.

BIRI GA does not use Apple Health data for advertising, targeted marketing or any purpose unrelated to Circadia and Sleep AI.
